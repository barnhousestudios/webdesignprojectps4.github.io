Hunter is seemingly one of the more calm characters. He can be chill at times, but he generally gets into plenty of shenanigans; whether it be by himself or with friends. Hunter and Robbie are both funny, but they are funny in their own way. He tends to sit back and watch Robbie or Playdo perform their antics. Is he a leader? When he wants to be.
