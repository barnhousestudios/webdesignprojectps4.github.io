Jason Cramer and Max Holsbeke were in lunch. They were in the 8th grade. Max had brought up the idea of making a television show. Jason was intrigued with the idea. They agreed to first make a shorter film, as practice, and then they would go on to make an actual series. And so came Farm Times. The boys decided to show the film to Adult Swim producers on a live stream, and the producers really liked it. After an exchange in some emails, they wanted more. Jason and Max decided it was time to finally make their awaited TV Show, Shenanigans.
