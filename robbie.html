Robbie and Hunter are both somewhat unintelligent. That being said, Robbie is something special. He is unaware of the world around him, living in his own little bubble. In this way, Robbie is innocent. He’s not overly generous, but deep down, he is a nice guy who wouldn’t hurt a fly, though he might act tough sometimes. He says weird things, listens to weird music, and is just a strange man in general. His uniqueness, though, makes him an entertaining person to watch.
